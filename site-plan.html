Planting club site plan

Planting Club

This name was chosen because it represents a community focused on planting, caring for plants, and sharing knowledge about nature and sustainability.

Site Purpose

The purpose of this website is to provide information about plants and how to take care of them, with a focus on Colombian plants. The site will also promote the activities of the Planting Club and encourage users to join the community.

Scenarios

  • How can I join the Planting Club?
  • What activities does the Planting Club offer?
  • What kind of plants can I grow in Colombia?

Color Scheme

  • Dark Green (#144d1d) Used for the header and footer backgrounds.
  • Light Green (#e8f5e9) Used as the main background color for the page.

Typography

  • Nunito: Used for all headings and body text.
